Codify v2.0

A brand new program to keep ahead of the competition

If it's broke, fix it

Codify Academy, an intensive sixteen-week front-end developer bootcamp in the heart of downtown San Francisco, was experiencing plateauing growth across a variety of KPIs including, most alarmingly, grad placement. The time had come for a comprehensive program rewrite and reboot. I effected and implemented the full curriculum rewrite, updated educational resources and activities, and content pivot for the changing demands of an evolved jobs market.

In addition to a brand new and focused learning management system, I effected sweeping new strategies for community engagement, alumni involvement, extra-curricular professional development tracks, and industry outreach. Internally, new metrics and tools were developed for onboarding and training future staff, gauging marketing performance, quantitatively assessing mentor performance, opening new critical feedback loops between students and staff, and building a more organic and far-reaching digital presence and following.

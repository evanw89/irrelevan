Encounters With The Imaginary

How many artists does it take to start a publishing house?

Encounters With the Imaginary Volume III cover image by Corrine Reid
Visual storytelling without angle or artifice

I'm one of three managing members and partners of Boneshaker Press, a publisher focused on giving voice to and generating opportunities for new and established artists. I've had my hands in its brand development and marketing, the development of its digital presence, community-building, project management, content-creation, content editing, and operations.

Encounters is Boneshaker's flagship yearly anthology of short creative fiction and accompanying illustrations from dozens of artists across the globe (myself included), each year 100% crowd-funded; but we also produce themed coloring books, sketchbooks, interviews, tutorials, artist spotlights, content aggregation, enamel pins, bookmarks, prints, and more.

It is by far one of the most rewarding collaborations to be a part of, in which I'm honored to have my work showcased, and with which I'm excited to continue working towards new and exciting projects!
